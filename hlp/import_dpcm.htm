Importing DPCM samples

It is possible to import wave files (PCM) and convert them to 1-bit DPCM samples. The format of PCM-samples should not care (but it must be raw PCM). Choose import in the DPCM instruments dialog, select a file and a new dialog will come up where you choose quality and volume.

Options

Quality (1 - 16)

  • 16 - Play the sample at normal speed using the highest pitch (15)
  • 1 - Play the sample at normal speed using the lowest pitch (0)

Volume (1 - 32)
Sets the volume level. Higher levels will remove noise.

Click-elimination
The volume of triangle and noise is decreased when a DPCM sample is playing, these two options will help restoring the volume after the sample is finished without causing a click.

  • Restore delta counter - Restores the channels delta counter by adding zeroes after the sample. Could cause a small echo.
  • Clip sample - Cuts the avaliable volume-range for the sample and leaves it at zero after end. Decreases volume and distorts the sample.

    What's best depends on the sample. If no option is choosen, a note-off will restore full volume (with a pop as result).

Max size of DPCM samples are 3.9 kB, at best quality (33 kHz) it's a little less than one second and lowest quality (4 kHz) about eight seconds.
