MIDI settings

Input Device
Choose the device from wich you want to record MIDI messages.

Master Sync
Scroll down when MIDI clock messages is received.

Do not record key releases
Disable recording of key-off messages.

Map MIDI to NES channels
Maps MIDI-channel messages to NES-channels, meaning channel 1 will be square 1, channel 2 is square 2 and so on. If not checked, the selected channel will receive all messages.

Record velocities
Translates key-velocity into a volume number, stored in the volume column.
